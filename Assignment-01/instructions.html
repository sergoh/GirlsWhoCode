Assignment 1

  1. Give your Webpage a Title by replacing the {{Title}} towards the top in the < title > tag. The browser uses this element to title your pages Tab, Check it out!
  2. Replace the {{Name}} with your name so your viewers can see who made this!
  3. Replace all the {{Thing}}'s and replace them with six of your favorite things
  4. Replace each {{This is my favorite because...}} with the apporpriate reason why that thing is one of your favorite things.
  5. Now the fun part. Use Google and find images for each of your favorite things. When you find an image, right click it and select "Copy image Address". This is what will tell your webpage where your image is located on the internet.

    On your page you will have < img > tags, each < img > tag has a "src" attribute,this is where your browser will know that images are located. Overwite http://tinyurl.com/gwc-placeholder and place the image address you got from Google.

    Each < img > will also have a "title" attribute, give a short description of your image and replace {{This is a....}}. This will show up when a user hovers over your image.
  6. Now that you have your webpage layed out, its time to customize it a little more. There are three things you can customize
    • Image Shape
    • The text color describing your favorite thing
    • Color around your favorite thing
